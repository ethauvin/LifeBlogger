LifeBlogger allows you to post your Nokia Lifeblog favorites to your blog.

Your images, text notes and videos can now be effortlessly posted to your blog using FTP (Blogger, etc.) or via the MetaWeblog API (Movable Type, Radio UserLand, etc.) [More...]

LifeBlogger makes extensive use of various open source libraries, including:

  • Thinlet
  • JReleaseInfo
  • Roxes Ant Tasks
  • Commons Net
  • JavaSQLite
  • Base64


  

LifeBlogger was based on a concept developed by:

  • Russell Beattie
  • Matt Croydon
  • and Erik C. Thauvin

The open source code is written and maintained by Erik C. Thauvin.
